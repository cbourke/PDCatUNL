Asynchronous Delay Demonstration

Sometimes slower operations are needed. Observe the first "disolve" effect below. It disolves the image from 100% to 99% to 98%, etc. down to 0% but does so as fast at the computer can do it. So fast that you don't even notice it and it appears to disolve instantaneously.

Now observe the second "disolve" effect. It too disolves the image from 100% to 99% to 98%, etc. down to 0% but does intentially causes a pause (of 0.01 seconds) on each step so that the effect takes 1 full second to fully disolve the image. This asynchronous delay makes the effect observable.

Sequential Effect


  repeat 100 times:
     disolve image by 1%

Asynchronous Effect


  repeat 100 times:
     disolve image by 1%
     wait for .01 seconds
